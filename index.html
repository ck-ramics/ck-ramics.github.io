OUR STORY

This is a story of hope, faith and dreams

My journey began in 2009 when I started teaching and creating my own ceramics full time. (Ecclesiastes 9:7) Coming from an artist filled family I claimed my spot as being a potter, teacher and business woman through grace. This is my passion. The people, the clay and the endless possibilities… Ck-ramics Pottery started off small and as years progressed so our seats filled with more students and our studio was recommended to host group activities by more & more companies. It hasn’t always been easy, I have faced many challenges, but we persevered and always looked up. In 2017 we opened our first shop & Pottery Painting Studio in Bellville. We are hoping to expand in 2018 and beyond. I am dreaming of new adventures!
